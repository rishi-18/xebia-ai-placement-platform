# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# AI-Powered Placement Management Platform

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** June 2026
**Document Owner:** Product Management Team
**Project Type:** Web-Based AI Placement Management System

---

# 1. Executive Summary

The AI-Powered Placement Management Platform is a centralized digital ecosystem designed to automate and optimize the end-to-end placement lifecycle for educational institutions.

The platform connects students, recruiters, placement coordinators, department heads, and institutional administrators through a single intelligent system that leverages Artificial Intelligence to improve placement outcomes, reduce administrative workload, and enhance recruiter engagement.

Key AI capabilities include:

* AI-Based Job Recommendations
* Resume Analysis & Improvement Suggestions
* Candidate Matching & Ranking
* Placement Readiness Insights
* Analytics & Reporting

The platform aims to replace fragmented placement workflows currently managed through spreadsheets, emails, WhatsApp groups, and manual reporting systems.

# 3. Background & Strategic Fit

Current placement processes suffer from:

* Lack of centralized information.
* Manual communication channels.
* Poor candidate-job matching.
* Time-consuming screening processes.
* Inaccurate reporting and analytics.

Students often struggle to identify suitable opportunities, recruiters spend significant effort screening candidates, and placement officers rely on disconnected tools for managing drives.

The proposed platform aligns with institutional goals of:

* Improving placement rates.
* Strengthening industry partnerships.
* Enhancing institutional reputation.
* Supporting accreditation and compliance requirements.

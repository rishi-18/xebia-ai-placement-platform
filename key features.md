## `# Key Features` 

```
AI-Powered Placement Management Platform
```

## `1. AI Resume Analysis` 

- `Upload resumes in PDF/DOC format.` 

- `Extract skills, education, and experience automatically.` 

- `Generate resume scores and improvement suggestions.` 

## `2. Personalized Job Recommendations` 

- `Recommend jobs based on skills, interests, CGPA, and profile data.` 

- `Match students with relevant placement opportunities.` 

## `3. Smart Candidate Matching` 

- `AI ranks candidates according to job requirements.` 

- `Helps recruiters identify suitable applicants quickly.` 

## `4. Placement Drive Management` 

- `Create and manage placement drives.` 

- `Define eligibility criteria such as CGPA, branch, and skills.` 

- `Notify eligible students automatically.` 

## `5. Student Dashboard` 

- `View job opportunities.` 

- `Track application status.` 

- `Monitor resume score and profile completion.` 

## `6. Recruiter Dashboard` 

- `Post job openings.` 

- `Search and filter candidates.` 

- `Schedule interviews and manage applications.` 

`7. Training & Placement Officer (TPO) Dashboard` 

- `Manage companies and placement drives.` 

- `Track student participation.` 

- `Generate placement reports and analytics.` 

## `8. Application Tracking System` 

- `Students can monitor each stage of recruitment:` 

- `Applied` 

- `Shortlisted` 

- `Interview Scheduled` 

- `Selected/Rejected` 

## `9. Interview Preparation Assistant` 

- `Generate AI-based interview questions.` 

- `Provide practice resources and preparation tips.` 

`10. Analytics and Reporting` 

- `Placement statistics and trends.` 

- `Student performance insights.` 

- `Company recruitment reports.` 

## `Benefits` 

- `Reduces manual work for placement officers.` 

- `Improves student-job matching.` 

- `Speeds up recruitment processes.` 

- `Enhances placement outcomes through AI-driven insights.`
